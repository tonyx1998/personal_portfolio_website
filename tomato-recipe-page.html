Tomato Egg Stir-fry

Tomato egg stir-fry is a common dish in China.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb tomato
  • 4 egg
  • 5 slice ginger
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 3 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Peel tomato and cut each tomato into 4 wedges
  2. Cut 5 thin slices of ginger
  3. Crack the eggs into a large bowl. Season it with salt (0.50 tsp), and then beat vigorously until well combined
  4. Heat the pan on high heat. Pour in the beaten eggs, and push the eggs gently around with a spatula to scramble.
  5. Add oil, ginger and tomato wedges to hot pan. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds, then add water (3 tbsp).
  6. Add the eggs back into the pan. Stir it in and let cook for 50 seconds, and serve.
